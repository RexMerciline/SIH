Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the weather predictions?

Our weather predictions are 85-90% accurate and are updated every 3 hours using satellite data and AI algorithms. We provide hyper-local forecasts specific to your farm location.

Can I use the voice assistant in my local language?

Yes! Our AI voice assistant supports Hindi, English, and will soon support regional languages like Punjabi, Marathi, Tamil, and Telugu. Simply speak naturally and it will understand your questions.

How do I get information about government schemes?

Use our 'Loans & Government Schemes' feature on the homepage. We provide updated information about all central and state government schemes, eligibility criteria, and application processes. You can also ask our AI assistant specific questions about schemes.

Is the service completely free?

Yes, all basic features including weather alerts, pest control guidance, market prices, and AI assistant are completely free. Some premium features like detailed soil analysis reports may have nominal charges.

How do I connect with local laborers?

Use our 'Labour Connect' feature to find skilled agricultural workers in your area. You can post your requirements, view worker profiles, check ratings, and directly contact them. All workers are verified for quality assurance.
